# Context Enrichment

## Purpose

Resolve explicit, material uncertainties for a promising Signal using the minimum additional context needed for a Gate decision.

## Required Inputs

- Signal and linked source evidence
- Specific unresolved questions
- Available thread, replies, quote posts, linked sources, or approved external sources
- Relevant reviewed knowledge-base records

## Required Outputs

Return structured answers per question with consulted sources, newly verified facts, model inferences, conflicting evidence, unresolved uncertainties, and a recommendation to re-evaluate, reject, or remain unresolved.

## Rejection Behavior

Stop when sources cannot answer the material question, are not attributable, or would only add background without changing the Gate decision. Do not broaden research speculatively and do not generate a draft.

## Evidence and Uncertainty Rules

Record provenance for every new claim. Separate what a source says from what is inferred. Never convert absence of evidence into confirmation, and never infer undocumented Ethplorer capabilities.
