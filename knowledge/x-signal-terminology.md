# X Signal Finder Terminology

These definitions are operational contracts for prompts, validation, storage, and editorial review. They are project-specific and must not be changed silently.

## Signal

A structured, evidence-linked representation of one potentially relevant discussion or event. A Signal may combine related source posts, but it is not yet an actionable recommendation and has not necessarily passed the Opportunity Gate.

## Opportunity

A structured recommendation created only after a Signal passes the Opportunity Gate. It identifies a real information gap, the audience benefit, the exact documented Ethplorer asset or capability that can close the gap, supporting evidence and uncertainty, and an appropriate action type. An Opportunity may contain a draft, but it is not itself a draft.

## Opportunity Gate

The mandatory evaluation between a Signal and an Opportunity. It tests whether a real information gap exists, whether Ethplorer has a specific documented and credible advantage in closing it, whether the audience benefits, whether evidence is sufficient, and whether participation avoids forced promotion. Its outcomes are accepted, rejected, or unresolved. Only accepted Signals may become Opportunities or receive drafts.

## Information Gap

A specific, audience-relevant question, missing fact, unsupported claim, absent measurement, or incomplete interpretation in an existing discussion that can be stated independently of Ethplorer. The ability to mention a product is not an information gap.

## Natural Relevance

The condition in which a specific, documented Ethplorer asset or capability directly answers the existing information gap and benefits the discussion's audience without changing the subject or forcing a marketing connection.

## Event Cluster

A set of source posts judged to concern the same underlying event, claim, entity, or tightly related discussion within a meaningful time window. A cluster preserves every member post and the evidence for grouping; it may support one or more Signals.

## Opportunity Card

The human-review representation of an Opportunity. It concisely presents the Signal, information gap, evidence, exact matching asset, Gate rationale, uncertainty, proposed action, optional draft, and optional Visual Brief without hiding provenance.

## Visual Brief

An optional, short specification for a visual that materially improves explanation or evidence. It states the communication goal, audience, facts or data to show, format, sources, uncertainty, and constraints. It is not an automatically generated image.

## Editorial Decision

A recorded human outcome for an Opportunity or draft, such as accept, reject, edit, defer, or request more evidence. It includes the responsible reviewer, time, rationale, and changes where available. It does not imply publication.

## Watching Service

A monitored product, protocol, organization, account, topic, or infrastructure service whose developments may create relevant Signals. Inclusion on a watch list raises collection or review attention but does not satisfy the Opportunity Gate.

## Portfolio Tracker

A product or capability that monitors the composition and changes of a set of blockchain assets or addresses over time. This term is a category label only; any claim about a specific Ethplorer implementation must be supported by the reviewed knowledge base.
